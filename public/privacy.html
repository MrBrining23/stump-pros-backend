Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Stump Pros WV ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website stumpproswv.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, and email address when you request a quote or contact us
  • Property information — address or location details needed to assess and complete a job
  • Lead source information — how you heard about us (e.g., Facebook, website, referral)
  • Communications — messages you send us via email, text, or our website

We do not collect payment card information directly — payments are processed securely through third-party payment processors.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide quotes and perform stump grinding services
  • Contact you about your service request or appointment
  • Send invoices and process payments
  • Follow up on completed work
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers — tools we use to run our business (scheduling, invoicing, communication) who are bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our rights

4. Meta / Facebook Lead Ads

We run advertising on Facebook and Instagram through Meta's platform. If you submit your information through a Meta lead form or ad, Meta's own Privacy Policy applies to that data collection. We receive your name and contact details from Meta solely for the purpose of following up on your service inquiry.

5. SMS and Text Messaging

If you opt in to receive text messages from us (for appointment reminders, quotes, or updates), we will only text you for those purposes. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from us.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

7. Data Retention

We retain customer information for as long as necessary to provide services and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes (typically 3–5 years). You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
